What are the categories?

There are many different categories, the one's relevant for your this placement are listed here.
Please note that you only need to qualify for one of the categories.

Accredited Investor

You qualify as an Accredited Investor if you meet at least one of the following criteria.

  • At least $1 million in financial assets (cash and marketable securities) before taxes, net of any debts. Neither your home nor any other real estate you own are considered financial assets.
  • Net income before taxes of more than $200,000 consistently over the past two years ($300,000 when combined with a spouse's net income).
  • Net assets of at least $5 million. Net assets exclude all liabilities.
Friends & Family, Business Associate

You qualify for this exception if you are a friend or family or business associate of a principal person.

Principal is either a director, executive officer or a major shareholder in the company.

You qualify as a friend if you know a principal of the company for enough time to be able to judge the person’s capabilities and trustworthiness. The relationship must be a direct first-degree friendship, it is not enough to belong to the same organization, association or religious group.

You qualify as family if you are a spouse, grandparent, sibling, child, grandchild or in-law of the principal. You don’t qualify as a family member if you are a cousin or anything other than what was listed.

You qualify as a business associate if you have had previous business dealings with a company principal to be able to judge the person’s capabilities and trustworthiness. You do not qualify if you were a customer, former client and definitely not simply a brief acquaintance.

Previous security holder exception

You qualify for this exception if you have invested previously in this company and you actively securities in the company itself.
